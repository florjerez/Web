— About the artist

Florencia
Jerez.

Argentine artist, Porto-based.

My inspiration mainly comes from nature. I observe even the simplest details — plants, light, textures, and color. Painting becomes a space where I let go, allowing emotions to move freely through gesture and take form on the canvas.

I explore the contrasts that coexist within us: calm and intensity, fragility and strength, simplicity and depth.

— The work

Every painting begins with observation — a plant, a shaft of light, the texture of bark. These quiet details accumulate until they demand to be expressed.

— The process

Working in acrylic on large-format canvas, each piece is built through layers of gesture and wash, allowing forms to surface and dissolve until the composition finds its own resolution.
